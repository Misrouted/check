Case 1
🤖 Generative AI Adoption Program
Strategy, prototyping, and team enablement for conversational AI
AI Strategy LLM / RAG Prototyping Enablement Architecture

L1 Service Desk was saturated. L2 agents spent hours on repetitive queries. No AI strategy existed — nobody had explored technical feasibility.

Case 3
⚡ Strategic Provisioning Automation
Autonomous system architecture to eliminate manual intervention
Automation Architecture SQL / ETL Data Governance
Context
Manual, slow, error-prone provisioning of external users on shared SharePoint systems. Hours of operational work every week.
My leadership
I designed a comprehensive automation architecture: migration to a relational SQL engine, ETL pipelines for ingestion and processing, secure credential generation, and automated notifications. Established process governance for traceability and compliance.
Impact
Provisioning went from hours of manual intervention to fully autonomous execution with zero errors. Exponential improvement in operational efficiency.

Case 4
📁 IT Operations Optimization
Tooling unification and bottleneck elimination through technical leadership
Optimization Tooling Strategy Automation
Context
Critical backlog in network permissions management. Operations depended on two virtual machines, creating a bottleneck that paralyzed the support team.
My leadership
I analyzed the network flow, designed a strategy to externalize permission management, and led the development of a unified application that eliminated VM dependency. Redesigned the team's workflow and enabled agents to operate autonomously.
Impact
Immediate backlog reduction of over 80%. The team went from being blocked by technical bottlenecks to operating with high efficiency and full autonomy.
